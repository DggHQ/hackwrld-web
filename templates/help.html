How to

Hackwrld is a multiplayer idle game made by theneedyguy where you run a command center. You passively "mine" coins and upgrade your components. The components help you to defend your command center from attacks from other players.

Firewall

The firewall has multiple purposes. It hides your components levels and your funds from other players and reduces the money lost from attacks from other players.

When a player scans the network two things may happen:

  • If they have a scanner with a lower level than your firewall they will just see all the values and funds as 0.
  • If their scanner has a higher level than your firewall they see all your components levels and how many coins you have.

When a player tries to steal from you two things may happen:

  • If they have a scanner with a lower level than your firewall, no money will be lost.
  • If their scanner has a higher level than your firewall they will receive a certain amount of money based on the level difference between their stealer and your firewall. The specific calculation is described in the "Stealer" section.

Scanner

The scanner helps you identify potential targets you may steal from. Each scan costs 0.1 * the level of your scanner. You will see all players and their component's levels & their current funds and cooldowns but depending on their firewall level, these values will be 0. All scans are stored in a list for target selection for use with the stealer.

Miner

The miner generates coins to be spent on upgrades, scans and actions. Per second you earn 0.001 * the level your miner.

Stealer

The stealer lets you steal money from other players. Targets are selected from the "Scanned Targets" list. Each steal costs 0.1 * the level of the stealer.

After a successful steal, the target will receive a cooldown which helps them recover from a steal. The cooldown is always 10 minutes after a successful steal.

If the defender's firewall level is higher than the stealer, 0 coins will be earned but the cost of the steal will be paid.

If the attacker has a higher stealer level than the defender's firewall level the following things will happen:

  • ((0.01 * the attacker's stealer level) * the level difference between the attacker's stealer and the defender's firewall) will be stolen.

So if you have a with level 50 and the target has a firewall with level 20 the attacker will steal ((0.011 * 50) * (50-20)) coins in total. The attacker will gain 16.5 coins, the defender will lose 16.5.

Since the attacker paid 5 coins to attack (because of the level 50 stealer) the net coins gained will be 11.5.

If the defender does not have enough coins to lose, the attacker will instead gain all the defender's coins. Leaving the defender empty handed.

Vault

The Vault is your source of money to buy upgrades for all components and to execute actions like stealing and scanning. The Vault capacity starts at 10 coins. The capacity doubles with each level.
